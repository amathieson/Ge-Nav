About this app
GéNav is a project that was started originally in late 2016 by the lead developer Adam Mathieson, he started creating the app because there where no really good apps that worked exactly the way he wanted. The version that was originally created was written in JAVA and would run as a native Android app, this proved to be very difficult and time consuming to update, so in July of 2017 Adam and Deyan (one of his friends) decided to change it over to an HTML5 and JavaScript app, using Adobe PhoneGap to build the app into the native apps that you know of today. The system is heavily based on the local public transport APIs (TPG Open Data), as well as the unofficial SBB Open Data APIs, the backend system combines all this data with data that was meticulously collected by hand by our developers as well as the Beta testers, who helped a lot with gathering images of around 90% of the TPG (and it's subcontractors) fleet.
